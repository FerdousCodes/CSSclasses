Join us for our first one-day event – it’s free!

When: Sunday, August 17, 10:30am - 6pm
Where: co.up, Adalbertstraße 7, 10999 Berlin.

RSVP

The day will include an optional workshop for beginners (and anyone who feels like refreshing their basics), as well as free hacking and collaborating for experts and newbies alike. Around 6pm, we’ll wrap up and everyone is invited to demo their achievements. Afterwards, we’ll end the day with celebratory drinks and socializing at co.up!
More infos and the learning materials will be made available soon.

Who can attend?

Anyone! Learners of all levels are welcome, including beginners with absolutely no experience and advanced CSS coders.
We only ask you to respect our core values, and to bring your own laptop.

An event around CSS… really?

Yes! We believe that CSS is one of the quickest and most fun ways to understand how websites are built and get your hands into coding. And that doesn’t mean CSS is for beginners only: Just check out the crazy experiments on Codepen.io or see what leading developers are discussing at events like CSSconf EU to get inspired and see how far you can take the language.

Who is organizing this?

The volunteer teams behind CSSconf EU and Open Tech School. We have experience in teaching workshops and organzing events, and our motivation is to share knowledge and passion for building fun and beautiful things for the web. We subscribe to the core values of Open Tech School and are committed to create a safe and encouraging space for learners and coaches.
