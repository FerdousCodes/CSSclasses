Join us for our first event – it’s free!

When: Sunday, August 17, 10:30am - 6pm
Where: co.up, Adalbertstraße 7, 10999 Berlin.

RSVP

The day will include a workshop for beginners (and anyone who needs to refresh their basics), as well as free hacking and collaborating for experts and newbies alike. The day will end with a demo of everyone’s achievements – and afterwards, celebratory drinks and socializing!

An event around CSS… really?

Yes! We believe that CSS is one of the quickest and most fun ways to understand how websites are build and get your hands into coding. And that doesn’t mean CSS is for beginners only: Just check out the crazy experiments on Codepen.io or see what leading developers are discussing at events like CSSconf EU to get inspired and see how far you can take the language.

Who is organizing this?

The volunteer teams behind CSSconf EU and Open Tech School. We have experience in teaching workshops and organzing events, and our motivation is to share knowledge and passion for building fun and beautiful things for the web. We subscribe to the core values of Open Tech School and are working hard to create a safe and encouraging space for learners and coaches.
